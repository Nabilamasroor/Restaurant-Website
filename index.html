Our Story

A Kitchen Built on Tradition

Bella Notte was born from a single obsession: recreating the honest, soul-warming food of Italian grandmothers. Every recipe is a love letter to the region it comes from — the rolling hills of Tuscany, the sun-drenched coasts of Amalfi, the bustling piazzas of Rome.

Our chef Marco Esposito trained in Naples for seven years before bringing his craft here. Everything is made fresh daily — pasta rolled by hand, sauces simmered for hours, bread baked each morning.

  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Wood-fired oven, authentic flavour
  • Family recipes, passed down for generations
